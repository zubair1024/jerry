WHAT IS TOMS?

The Tank Operations Management System (TOMS) product solution monitors, analyzes and tracks the various parameters of stationary as well as mobile tank assets. The solution contains various alarms, dashboards and reports that can be customized as required. You can monitor the storage of liquids as a part of this solution. Through the application, you can monitor tanks in terms of volume, and can track mobile assets such as tanker heads and attached tankers. The information collected is used in various BI reports and dashboards that provide relevant and real time data.
